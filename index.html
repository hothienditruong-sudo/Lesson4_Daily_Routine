📝 Ngữ pháp trọng tâm
Present Simple for Daily Routines + Frequency Adverbs

1️⃣ Present Simple — Cấu trúc

Khẳng định (+):   I / You / We / They + Verb (nguyên mẫu)  |  He / She / It + Verb + s/es
Phủ định (−):   I/You/We/They + don't + V  |  He/She/It + doesn't + V
Câu hỏi (?):   Do + I/You/We/They + V?  |  Does + He/She/It + V?
💡 Ví dụ minh hoạ
✅ I wake up at 7 a.m. every day.
✅ She goes to work at 8 a.m.
✅ He watches TV in the evening.
❌ I don't watch TV in the morning.
❌ She doesn't eat breakfast at home.
❓ Do you work on weekends? — Yes, I do. / No, I don't.
❓ Does your mother cook every day? — Yes, she does.
⚠️ Quy tắc thêm -s / -es / -ies
• Thông thường: + s  →  works, eats
• Tận cùng -ch/-sh/-s/-x/-o: + es  →  watches, goes
• Tận cùng phụ âm + y: đổi y → ies  →  studies, carries

2️⃣ Frequency Adverbs — Trạng từ tần suất

Vị trí: TRƯỚC động từ chính  |  SAU động từ "be"

AdverbTần suấtVí dụ
Always
100%		I always brush my teeth.
Usually
~80%		She usually wakes up at 7.
Often
~60%		He often has a snack.
Sometimes
~40%		We sometimes eat out.
Rarely
~20%		I rarely stay up late.
Never
0%		She never skips breakfast.
📌 Vị trí:
✅ I always wake up early.    ✅ She is usually happy.
❌ I wake up always early.    ❌ Always she wakes up early.

💬 Mẫu hội thoại luyện tập
👤 A — Interviewer
What time do you usually wake up in the morning?
👤 B — Speaker
I usually wake up at 7 o'clock. I don't get up straight away — I stay in bed for a few minutes first!
👤 A
What do you do in the morning before work?
👤 B
First, I have a shower. Then I clean my teeth and put on a bit of make-up. After that, I have breakfast — usually coffee and toast.
👤 A
Do you usually cook dinner at home?
👤 B
Sometimes. But on busy days my mum doesn't bother to cook, so we order a takeaway. It's quick and delicious!
👤 A
What do you usually do in the evening?
👤 B
I usually stay in and watch TV. At weekends I sometimes go out or eat out. I always go to bed at about 10:30 pm and go to sleep straight away.
